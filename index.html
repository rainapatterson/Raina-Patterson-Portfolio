Hiya! I'm Raina!

Web Designer, Developer, & Creative.

Design is my tool for sparking connection and a sense of community.

I care about people. How they feel, how I can help them, and how we can work together to become better.

My years of being a supervisor at Starbucks have taught me how much people desire a sense of community and how something like a cup of coffee can accomplish that desire.

For me, creating user-centered designs is my "coffee" for bringing people together to help them feel included, acknowledged, and appreciated.
