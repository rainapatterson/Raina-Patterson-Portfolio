Web Designer, Developer, & Creative.

I have a passion for bringing people together through design & arts.

Raina uses design as her tool for sparking connection.

Her expereince leading and guiding teams as a supervisor have given her the skills necessary for effective collaboration, communication, and inspiring growth in herself and those around her.
